Camp Guidelines

Attendance for all sessions is compulsory.

Each delegate is permitted to register for only one instrument track.

Dress appropriately and let your clothing be modest.

Mobile Usage - carefully go through the mobile usage instructions shared below 📱.

Indecent behavior, inappropriate attire, or substance abuse will not be tolerated and will result in immediate disqualification from the workshop.

Mobile Phone Usage Policy & Parental Consent Form

Dear Delegates and Parents,

Greetings in the name of the Lord. The Enrich Workshop Team is pleased to welcome all delegates to Enrich Worship Camp 2026. In order to maintain a focused, disciplined, safe, and spiritually enriching atmosphere, all delegates are required to follow the mobile phone policy stated below.

Mobile Phone Usage Policy

  • Mobile phones may be used only during the designated break time: 5:00 PM to 6:00 PM (subject to change based on the workshop schedule).

  • Outside the permitted time, all mobile phones must be switched off and submitted to the designated coordinator or concerned person.

  • Delegates are expected to use mobile phones responsibly and only in a manner consistent with the values and discipline of the workshop.

  • In case of an emergency or genuine necessity, exceptions may be permitted at the discretion of the workshop leadership.

  • Designated Pastors and Volunteers will be available at all times to assist delegates who need to contact their parents or guardians outside the permitted phone usage period.

Parental Consent for Delegates Under 18 Years of Age

For all delegates below the age of 18, parental or guardian consent is required to confirm understanding of and agreement with the mobile phone policy.
